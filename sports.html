FAQ

  • Do I need any preparation for snorkeling lessons?

    • If your swimming skills aren’t the best, take some swimming lessons at your local pool. And even if you’re a decent swimmer, becoming an even better swimmer will only benefit you in the water.

  • Is it necessary to be fit to take lessons?

    • It is not important that you do strenuous weightlifting and similar heavy stuff. A little cardio and freehand exercise should be enough. Do endurance exercises to increase your stamina so that you can stay in the water for a longer period. You can also use some yoga for flexibility.

  • Should I ride solo or tandem on a kayak?

    • Tandem kayaking can be tricky if the two paddlers are sitting closer together and end up hitting each other’s paddles if their strokes are out of sync. But, if you’re paddling with a child in front this isn’t normally an issue.
